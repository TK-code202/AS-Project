PetHome Animal Shelter

PetHome is an animal shelter which takes care of animals and offers them for adoption. The shelter was established as an initiative to provide care to several animals around the world. PetHome was established in the year 2014 by Dr Fola Williams who is a veterinarian. Her love for animals led her to establish this animal shelter. PetHome offers a range of services which include : health care, pet foods etc. The Animal Shelter has many good facilities for the smooth running of the shelter. The shelter offers many animals for adoption. Every animal put up for adoption undergoes a series of tests to ensure they are compatible with humans. To adopt a pet, an extensive form has to be filled to ensure the person adopting will take good care of the animals. Also, a PetHome account is required be allowed to adopt an animal. To sign up, check out our home account and a sign up button will be seen. PetHome is registered and licensed to operate by the Federal Governmnt. PetHome has ,any centers in Nigerian major cities like Lagos, Ibadan, Port Harcourt and Abuja. Click Visit Shelter at the footer of the Page to find out the shelter closest to you. PetHome Animal Shelters are open for 5 hours(from 9am to 2pm). To learn more about adopting a pet, check out POLICY at the foot of the webpage.
